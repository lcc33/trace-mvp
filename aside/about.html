About TraceVault

TraceVault is a modern lost and found system designed to help users recover lost items and report found belongings with ease. Whether you've misplaced your phone, found someone's wallet, or want to keep track of your devices, TraceVault is here to help.

Key Features

  • Report Found Items: Quickly report any found items so owners can locate them.
  • Explore & Search: Browse and search for lost items reported by others in your area.
  • Device Tracing: Connect your devices (phone, PC, iPad, etc.) to your account and trace their location if they go missing.

Our Mission

Our mission is to reunite people with their lost belongings and provide peace of mind through secure device tracing. TraceVault leverages technology to create a helpful, community-driven platform for everyone.
